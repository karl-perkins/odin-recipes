Cheesy Tomato Pasta

Description

A quick and easy cheesy tomato pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Cream cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in water over high heat until cooked
  2. Heat oil in pan over medium-high heat
  3. Fry diced onions until translucent
  4. Fry crushed garlic until fragrant
  5. Add tomatoes and Italian seasoning, and simmer until the sauce had thickened
  6. Add cream cheese, and allow to melt
  7. Add drained pasta, and stir until the pasta has been coated in sauce
  8. Serve with grated parmesan and black pepper
