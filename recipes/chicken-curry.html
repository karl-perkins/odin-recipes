Chicken Curry

Description

A quick and easy tomato-based chicken curry which can be served with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Onion
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Capsicum
  • Tumeric
  • Cumin
  • Chili
  • Garam Masala

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast into 1 inch cubes
  2. Combine 1 teaspoon tumeric, 1 and 1 half teaspoons cumin, 1 teaspoon chili powder and 1 teaspoon garam masala with the cubed chicken breasts and let marinate for at least 1 hour prior to cooking
  3. Add oil to frying pan over medium-high heat
  4. Add diced onions and fry until slightly translucent
  5. Add 1 teaspoon tumeric and 1 teaspoon cumin
  6. Add crushed garlic cloves and ginger paste and fry until fragrant
  7. Add marinated chicken and fry until browned
  8. Add canned diced tomatoes and diced capsicum, and simmer over medium-low heat until chicken is cooked through
