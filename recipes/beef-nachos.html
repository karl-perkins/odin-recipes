Beef Nachos

Description

Quick and easy beef nachos

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spice mix
  • Tortila chips
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Canned kidney beans
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Jalapeno peppers

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan over medium-high heat
  2. Fry diced onions until slightly translucent
  3. Fry crushed garlic until fragrant
  4. Fry minced beef until browned
  5. Add tomatoes and kidney, and simmer over medium-low heat until beef is cooked
  6. Serve on a layer of tortila chips and top with grated cheese, a dollop of sour cream, and sliced jalapeno peppers
